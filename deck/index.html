PART 4 · CASE STUDY

Yudi on Xiaohongshu 小红书成长历程

How an E-H therapist built trust outside the mainstream · 在主流平台之外，走出一条路

1
Why Xiaohongshu · 为什么选择小红书
Algorithm actively promotes emotional content · Existential narrative is a natural fit for the "emotional notes" format · Direct client connection
算法扶持情感内容，存在主义叙事天然适合「情绪笔记」格式，直接连接来访者
2
The Deepest Overlap Between Content Creators and Therapists: Presence with Awareness in Relationship · 自媒体博主和心理咨询师的最大共性：带着觉察在关系之中在场
Identity Positioning (Self-Identity) — what · Choosing labels through self-knowledge: personal identity, professional identity, content creator identity
身份定位（自我认同）— what · 通过对自我的认识了解选择标签；个人身份、职业身份、自媒体博主身份 Emotional Expression (Mode of Presence) — how · We show up through language, images, and video; how we describe things — what we emphasize — reveals who we are
情感类表达（存在方式）— how · 我们通过语言、图片和短视频呈现的方式在场；描述事情的方式和重点会让对方看到我们是什么样的人
3
Writing with Emotion as a Way of Being · 用情感来写作，是一种存在方式
Writing is not a tool for expressing thought — it is the way existence reveals itself (beneath thought, feeling is always already present) · When you write, you are not merely recording yourself; you are being-with yourself, and with the world · Born, necessarily, from incompleteness, irrationality, and failure — just like therapy
写作不是表达思想的工具，而是存在自身显现的方式（思想背后是情感的现身）· 当你"写"，你不仅在记录自己，而是在与自己、与世界共在 · 注定从不完全、不完整、不理性和失败之中诞生（和心理咨询一样）
4
The more personal, the more universal. · 最个人的，就是最普遍的。
